# BOLOs & Charges

Two related LEO systems that write to a person's record and surface across every terminal.

## BOLOs

A **BOLO** (Be On the Look-Out) alerts units to watch for a person or vehicle.

<Steps>

### Create

From the MDT or LASD CAD, create a BOLO against a **name** or **plate** with a reason. It is stored
server-side and broadcast to all on-duty units.

### Hit on query

When any unit runs a matching person or plate query, the BOLO surfaces as a **hit** on the result —
and the CCTV scanner shows a **BOLO ACTIVE** flag.

### Cancel

When resolved, cancel the BOLO so it stops hitting.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  BOLOs are shared across the LAPD, LASD and Agency terminals — creating one anywhere is visible
  everywhere, including the LASD CAD.
</Callout>

## Charges & citations

Charges are driven by a configurable **penal code**. From a person's record an officer can:

- **Apply charges** — pick one or more offences from the penal code, each with its reference and
  severity. Charges attach to the subject's record.
- **Issue a citation** — a lighter, fine-style entry (e.g. `Speeding 90/50`) for infractions that
  don't warrant a full arrest.

Both are written to the record and appear in the MDT query, the person card, and the CCTV scanner's
MDT summary.

### Penal code

The penal code lives in the charges config, so you can tailor offences, categories and severities to
your server's legal system. Each charge carries a title, category and reference used when it is
applied.

## Where they show up

| Surface | Shows |
| --- | --- |
| MDT / CAD query | BOLO hits + charges/citations on the record |
| Dispatch console | BOLO list |
| Person card | Charges, citations, warrant status |
| [CCTV scanner](/features/cctv) | BOLO ACTIVE / ACTIVE WARRANT flags + recent records |

<Callout type="info">
  A person's **active warrant** (set on their profile) also surfaces everywhere charges do, including
  a red **ACTIVE WARRANT** flag on the CCTV scan card.
</Callout>
