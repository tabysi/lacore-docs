# BOLOs & Charges

## BOLOs

A **BOLO** (Be On the Look-Out) is created from the MDT or the LASD CAD against a name or plate. It
is stored server-side, broadcast to on-duty units, and **surfaces as a hit** when someone runs a
matching person or plate query. BOLOs can be cancelled when resolved.

## Charges & citations

Charges are driven by a configurable **penal code**. From a person's record an officer can:

- apply one or more **charges** (each with its penal-code reference and severity), or
- issue a **citation** (a lighter, fine-style entry).

Both are written to the subject's record and appear in the MDT query and person card. The penal
code lives in the charges config so you can tailor it to your server's legal system.

<Callout type="info">
  BOLOs and charges are shared across the LAPD, LASD and Agency terminals — creating one in any of
  them is visible in the others.
</Callout>

## Where they show up

| Surface | Shows |
| --- | --- |
| MDT query | BOLO hits + charges/citations on the record |
| Dispatch console | BOLO list |
| Person card | Charges, citations, warrant status |
| CCTV scanner | BOLO ACTIVE / ACTIVE WARRANT flags + recent records |
