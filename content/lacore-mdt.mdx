## What's included

- **4 Mobile Data Terminals** on one shared backend — LAPD PremierOne, LASD PCMS, Agency/CHP, EMS/Fire.
  The right terminal opens automatically by department; data is shared across all of them.
- **Live Dispatch console** — real GTA tile map, editable zones & districts, click-to-assign, unit
  management, CCTV markers.
- **CCTV surveillance** — fixed cameras + placeable field cameras with a targeting scanner; auto-grouped
  by locality.
- **BOLOs, charges & citations** — a configurable penal code; fines & jail computed server-side.
- **Evidence & reports** — structured reports and tagged evidence with visibility-gated records.
- **Multi-character profiles** — a persistent person/vehicle "digital file" the MDT reads from (on ESX,
  characters come straight from the framework).
- **Corrections/Jail, Impound, K9 and an Air Unit heli-cam.**
- **Big Brother logging, ACE permissions and a hardened security stack.**
- Fully localised **EN / DE / RU**; all config files are escrow-open.

## Requirements

- **oxmysql**, **pma-voice**, **spawnmanager** (same as the core). oxmysql auto-creates its tables and
  falls back to local JSON if unavailable.
- Standalone, or an **ESX / QBCore / QBox** bridge — no framework required.

## Install

<Callout type="info">
  Escrow-protected, IP-locked to one server (one purchase = one server).
</Callout>

1. Download the resource from your Tebex purchase and unzip it into `resources` as `lacore-mdt`.
2. Ensure the dependencies **before** it, then the resource, in `server.cfg`:
   ```cfg
   ensure oxmysql
   ensure pma-voice
   ensure lacore-mdt
   ```
3. **Plug-and-play** — it starts and lets officers in with no Discord/whitelist setup. Grant access via
   the `lacore_mdt.access` ACE (or map an ESX/QB job), then open with `/mdt`.
