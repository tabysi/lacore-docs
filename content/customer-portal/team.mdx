# Team

Run your community with others? **Dashboard → Team** lets you give trusted teammates **scoped** access
to your account — without handing over your login.

<Steps>

### Invite a teammate

Add them by their **Discord user ID** and pick which areas they may touch.

### Choose their scopes

| Scope | Grants access to |
|-------|------------------|
| **Servers** | Servers & license keys |
| **Config** | Server config |
| **Billing** | Payments |

### They switch into your account

Your teammate signs in with **their own** Discord, then picks your account from a switcher in their
sidebar. They only ever see the areas your scopes allow.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  **You keep control.** Every action is re-checked on the server against the scopes you granted — the
  account switcher alone grants nothing. Change or remove a teammate's access any time; it applies
  immediately.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  Finding a Discord user ID: enable **Developer Mode** in Discord (Settings → Advanced), then
  right-click a user → **Copy User ID**.
</Callout>
