# Customer Portal

Your LACORE account lives at **[lacore.netica.dev/dashboard](https://lacore.netica.dev/dashboard/)**.
Sign in with the **Discord account that owns your licence** — no separate password, no new account to
create. Your role in our Discord decides what you see.

<Callout type="info">
  **How access works.** The portal reads your roles in the LACORE Discord: a **customer** role opens
  the customer dashboard, **staff / owner** roles open the admin console, and a **partner** role adds
  the Partner page. Nothing to configure — just sign in.
</Callout>

## Roles at a glance

| Role | Sees |
|------|------|
| **Customer** | Servers · License keys · Server config · Payments · Downloads · Support |
| **Partner** | The above **+** the Partner page (referral stats & earnings) |
| **Team member** | Whatever the account owner grants — scoped to *their* account |
| **Staff / Owner** | The admin console (servers, bans, tickets, bot control, settings) |
