# Partner program

Partners earn a share of the sales they refer. If you have the **partner role**, **Dashboard →
Partner** shows your performance.

## What you see

| Tile | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Referred sales** | Number of purchases attributed to your referral code |
| **Referred revenue** | Total value of those sales |
| **Your earnings** | Your payout — a percentage of the net revenue |

Your **referral code** is shown at the top of the page (with a copy button), along with your payout
rate.

<Callout type="info">
  **How attribution works.** Sales are attributed by your **referral code** on each Tebex order — an
  admin assigns you a code + payout rate when you join the program. Stats update as new orders come in.
</Callout>

## Becoming a partner

The program is invite-only. Interested in referring LACORE (content creator, community, reseller)?
Reach out in the [Discord](https://discord.gg/b6YbvVEvYp).
