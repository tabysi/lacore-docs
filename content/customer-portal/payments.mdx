# Payments

**Dashboard → Payments** shows your LACORE purchases from the store — package, amount and status
(purchase, renewal, refund).

<Callout type="info">
  **How purchases are matched.** Payments are linked to your account by the **Discord ID you enter at
  checkout**. If a purchase is missing, make sure you provided your Discord ID on the Tebex checkout,
  then open a ticket and we'll link it.
</Callout>

Delivery of the resource itself continues through **Tebex & Keymaster**, tied to your purchase — see
[Downloads](https://lacore.netica.dev/dashboard/downloads/) in the dashboard for the current version.
