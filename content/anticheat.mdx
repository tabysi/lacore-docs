# Anti-Cheat & Security

LACORE ships with a **built-in, multi-layered anti-cheat** — protection is included with the resource.
There is no separate product to buy and nothing extra to wire up: enable it, set your punishment
levels, and your server is defended out of the box.

<Callout type="info">
  Protection is **on the house** — the anti-cheat is part of LACORE, not an add-on. It runs alongside
  everything else and stays fully standalone.
</Callout>

## What you're protected against

LACORE actively guards your server against the common ways cheaters ruin a roleplay experience:

- 🛡️ **Invincibility & health/armour tampering** — god-mode and impossible-stat exploits
- 🔫 **Illegal weapons & combat abuse** — banned weapons, aimbot-style behaviour and rapid-fire abuse
- 💥 **Explosion, projectile & crash exploits** — including screen-crash and lag attacks
- 🏃 **Movement cheats** — teleporting, speed and no-clip
- 🧨 **Mod menus & script injection** — known menu markers and injected-event abuse
- 📦 **Resource dumping** — headless "download-only" clients and dump bots
- ♻️ **Event spam** — mass-action floods (e.g. mass-cuff / dispatch spam), even from a compromised account
- 🕵️ **Anti-cheat tampering** — attempts to disable or strip out the protection itself

## Why it's effective

<Callout type="info">
  For security reasons we **don't publish exactly how each check works** — that would only help
  cheaters adapt. What matters for you is the design below.
</Callout>

- **Server-authoritative core.** The most important checks run **on the server**, so a cheater can't
  simply switch off the protection on their own machine.
- **Built for fair play.** A smart, self-balancing system means legitimate players going about normal
  roleplay are **not** punished for false alarms — real offenders are caught, honest players aren't.
- **Escalating, not trigger-happy.** Repeat offenders are handled decisively while one-off blips fade
  away, so you avoid the "banned my whole community by accident" trap.
- **Full control.** Every protection is **configurable** — choose log / kick / ban per detection, and
  whitelist your staff so admins are never caught by their own tools.
- **Evidence & logging.** Detections are logged to your Discord with the player's details (and can even
  attach a screenshot), so your team has a clear audit trail.
- **Constantly hardened.** The protection is maintained and expanded with every LACORE update.

## Recommended server hardening

The anti-cheat is strongest when paired with a few standard FiveM server settings. LACORE checks these on
start-up and reminds you in the console if any are missing — so you always know your server is locked
down. Add these to your `server.cfg`:

```ini
sv_scriptHookAllowed 0      # block ScriptHookV trainers (Menyoo, Simple Trainer, …)
sv_pureLevel 2              # accept only original client files (2 = strictest)
sv_enforceGameBuild 3258    # pin one game build so old-build exploits can't connect
sv_filterRequestControl 4   # filter entity-control requests (stops foreign-entity manipulation)
```

(Plus OneSync enabled, which the server-authoritative checks rely on.)

<Callout type="warning">
  No anti-cheat on any platform can promise to stop **100 %** of cheats — that's simply not how FiveM
  works. LACORE's job is to make cheating **hard, risky and quickly caught**, and to keep your honest
  players safe from false bans. Combined with the recommended server settings above, your community is
  well protected.
</Callout>
