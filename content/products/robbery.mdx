# Robbery

Small-time crime for your server — stores, ATMs and quick scores that give players a risk/reward
alternative to the legal grind, with a natural hook into police dispatch.

<Callout type="warning">
  **Coming soon.** This LACORE product is in development. This page is a placeholder — full features,

## The idea

Robberies are the bread-and-butter of criminal roleplay: fast, repeatable, and a great way to create
organic cops-and-robbers moments. LACORE Robbery aims to deliver that loop with sensible cooldowns and
dispatch integration so it drives roleplay instead of chaos.
