# Farming

A complete farming job for your server — a legal, grind-friendly money loop that gives new and
casual players something to do: work a field, process the yield, and sell it on.

<Callout type="warning">
  **Coming soon.** This LACORE product is in development. This page is a placeholder — full features,
  setup and pricing will be filled in at release.
</Callout>

## The idea

Farming is one of the classic "starter jobs" on a roleplay server: low barrier to entry, steady
income, and a natural on-ramp into the wider economy. LACORE Farming aims to package that loop cleanly
so you can drop it in without wiring together five separate scripts.
