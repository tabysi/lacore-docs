# 3.1.5 — CCTV Surveillance Suite, Scanner & Field Cameras

The CCTV system became a full surveillance suite.

## Added — CCTV camera viewer (`/cctv`)

- Fixed-position POV camera (WASD / arrows look, scroll zoom) with real area streaming (a clone ped
  stays at your desk while the hidden real ped is teleported to the camera so the area streams).
- Cameras grouped into networks, gated by job (`leo` / `security` / `all`).
- You cannot fire your weapon while operating a camera.

## Added — Targeting scanner

- Left-click raycasts a **person** or **vehicle** and runs an animated scan sequence (reticle,
  sweeping line, live percentage). Duration `CCTV.scanTime` (default 6 s).
- An info card follows the target on screen with a compact **mugshot** and, for registered people,
  an **MDT summary** (priors, active BOLOs, up to three recent records).
- Flags: **ACTIVE WARRANT**, **BOLO ACTIVE**, **REPORTED STOLEN**, **UNREGISTERED**.
- NPCs / unregistered targets resolve locally so a scan never hangs; the server only enriches
  registered players and plates.
- The operator's desk standin is a networked ped that resolves to the real player when scanned.

## Added — Field cameras + dispatch-map integration

- `/camtool` places cameras by shooting a spot; `/cameras` manages them (rename / delete / view /
  toggle tool).
- Placed cameras persist (`data/cctv_cams.json`) and appear under a "Field Cameras" network.
- The dispatch map shows all accessible cameras as 📹 markers; a "view this camera?" modal opens the
  live view in front of the console and reopens the console on exit.

## Changed

- **Turf / Gang-War is now opt-in** — `Turf.enabled = false` by default; `Turf.drawMarkers` off too.

## Fixed

- Could shoot while inside a camera (now hard-blocked).
- Dispatch console stayed in front of the live camera view.
- Dispatch map position tracking tightened to ~1 s updates.
